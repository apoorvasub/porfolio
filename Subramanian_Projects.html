Scratch-The Secret of Arcadia High School

Course:Computer Science and Software Engineering

Partner Project

8/29/17-9/7/17

My partner and I worked together to create our own game, The Secret of Arcadia High School, using our skills on Scratch. Our game is a virtual scavenger hunt in which the players have to find three keys using the given clues. Once they have found all the keys, they will be given a fourth and final clue about where the secret of Arcadia High School is hidden. Once the player finds the secret, they have won the game. We had difficulty coding some parts of the game, but were able to conduct a quick search on the internet, and consult our instructor to resolve any questions we had. In addition, in order to avoid the risk of our code breaking, we saved several early versions of our game. By the end of this project, I had successfully learned how to use Scratch.
