About Me

My name is Apoorva Subramanian, and I am currently a sophomore in Dublin High School. I aspire to contribute to the field of medicine by becoming a pediatrician. I am very passionate about this field because it combines my interests in biology, chemistry and health sciences. For as long as I could remember, becoming a doctor has always been a dream of mine, and I intend to pursue it.


In order to make my dream a reality, I plan on taking advanced courses in the sciences and mathematics, in addition to obtaining experience in the field. I hope to volunteer in a nearby hospital and shadow doctors to familiarize myself with the profession. In addition, I plan to be a high school intern in a University of California, San Franciso research facility. I am interested in attending universities such as University of California, Los Angeles and University of California, Davis.


The process to become a doctor is very strenuous and requires incredible commitment and perseverance. I believe that I am well suited for this career because I am a very driven individual and work very diligently to complete every task. I have demonstrated excellance in studies throughout my schooling. I have developed the ability to easily adapt and thrive in different environments. Some of my achievements include maintaining Honor Roll throughout middle school and getting a 4.0/4.0 unweighted grade point average during both semesters of freshman year in one of the top Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math schools in the nation. My experiences as a freshman revealed that I work well under pressure and in stressful situations. Over the last couple years, I have learned the importance of persistence, confidence and assertiveness. This is something I had struggled with when I was younger, but have improved in over the years.


Thank you for taking the time to read about me. Feel free to explore my website and follow along on my journey to pursuing my career.
